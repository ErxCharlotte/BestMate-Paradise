Introduction

Find out more about BestMate Paradise and us.

BestMate Paradise is an ideal, dog-oriented, humane, skilled, loving pet boarding and pet training facility for urban dog families.

We have been adhering to the service concept and corporate principle of "everything is based on integrity", and hired professional dog obedience trainers, dog behavior psychological guides and senior management teams. With exquisite professional technology and craftsman's meticulous spirit, we provide meticulous services for your pets.

Years of accumulation of experience, professional concepts, and good service awareness have not only been recognized by the owners and partners, but also won us a good reputation!

We have always been adhering to treat every pet owner with integrity. Our principle is that what you adopt to us at BestMate Paradise is not a pet dog, but a very important member of your family.Here, your family members will enjoy a beautiful and leisurely holiday-style life, such as physical and mental pleasure, sunshine, free running, making friends, accompanying people, scientific diet and so on.

Our team

Our team has professional pet trainers, pet groomers, pet trainers and pet medics and other professionals. At the same time, every staff member has a heart of love for pets, and we will try our best to bring the best service to pets. No difficulty can stop us from providing the best for our pets.

Our happiness lies in sharing the results with pets and their owners, sharing success with the team, and realizing the common improvement of the value of pets, owners and our team.

Company History

Since our establishment, we have been adhering to the original intention of treating pets with love, and we have adhered to this concept for nearly 10 years. In the past 10 years, we have experienced a lot, touched, gained and grown. As of April 2022, the number of family companion dogs who have spent time in BestMate Paradise has reached more than 2,600, and it has helped many dog-raising families to solve practical problems encountered in the process of raising dogs.

BestMate Paradise

We are committed to providing the best accommodation environment and service for pets, so that customers can trust us to take care of their pets when they go out. We will provide the best price and the best service for pets and provide diversified service types to customers' pets. Every staff member will take care of pets as his or her own family, so that pets will not feel lonely and bored.

You can choose the best fostering environment for pets here, and believe that our services will definitely bring the best to your pets.

    Company history

    BestMate Paradise was founded in 2022 by 5 students from the University of Sydney. At the beginning, they realized that people often need to go out in the current society, and their pets are difficult to be properly taken care of and handled.
